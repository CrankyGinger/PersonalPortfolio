About Me

Hello, my name is Robert, and this is a little about me. I am currently a student at Clovis East High school attending the Center for Advanced Research and Technology(CART). At CART, I am currently learning about front-end development, the making of webpages, and all the components that go into creating a good user experience for people browsing the web. I am in the class of 2022, and over the past four years of high school, I've had the opportunity to make some amazing things, especially at CART.

I enjoy doing a lot of things outside of school though. One of the many things I enjoy outside of school is reading. My favorite genre to read is by far medieval fantasy - things like 'A song of Ice and Fire', and 'The Witcher'. I also really enjoy weight-lifting. I am very new to weight-lifting, but I greatly enjoy the feel of training to failure, and the endorphin rush after a tough set is an amazing feeling.

Awards and Achievements

One of the awards I've recieved is the 'Students of Promise' scholarship. This is the only scholarship I've gotten, but getting a scholarship at all is huge. The scholarship gives me $2,000 that I can use at any college I want. To me the scholarship itself isn't the really important aspect of this achievement though, but rather what it represents, representing what can happen if you keep working and don't give up.

One of my achievements is Principal's honor roll. This is only given to people who had a 4.0 or above GPA at the end of the semester. This displays how much hard work and dedication a student has to school, as a very small amount of people get it.
